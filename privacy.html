Privacy Policy

Last updated:

1. Information We Collect

Discipline and Yoga is committed to protecting your privacy. When you enroll through our website, we collect the information you voluntarily provide: name, contact number, email address, and city. We do not collect sensitive personal data beyond what is necessary to provide our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Process your enrollment and create your personalized yoga plan
  • Communicate program updates, schedules, and support
  • Improve our services based on community feedback
  • Send occasional wellness tips (you can opt out anytime)

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All data transmissions are encrypted using industry-standard protocols.

4. Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience and basic analytics to understand how visitors interact with our site. You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Google Analytics may collect anonymous usage data; please review Google's privacy policy for details.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services for email delivery, form processing, or analytics. These partners are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal obligations)
  • Opt out of non-essential communications at any time

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for individuals aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy occasionally to stay informed about how we protect your information.
